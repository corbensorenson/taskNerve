# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or doxxing.
- Deliberate sabotage of collaboration workflows.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.
